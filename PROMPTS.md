# AI prompt iterations

## Prompt variant 1 — balanced
- Goal: produce an easy-to-read summary plus a warm reply.
- Prompt: Ask the model to summarise the review and suggest a polite reply.
- Result: worked best for everyday guest feedback because it balanced detail and warmth without being too verbose.

## Prompt variant 2 — concise
- Goal: keep the output short and direct.
- Prompt: ask for a short summary and one short reply.
- Result: useful for quick replies, but sometimes lost helpful nuance.

## Prompt variant 3 — insightful
- Goal: surface themes and deeper sentiment.
- Prompt: ask the model to highlight themes, sentiment, and offer a thoughtful reply.
- Result: strong for analysis, but slightly too dense for the simple dashboard workflow.

## Best choice
The balanced prompt performed best overall because it gave a clear summary and a polished reply while staying concise enough for the UI.
