# AI Prompt Iterations

## Prompt Variant 1 — Balanced

### System Prompt
You are a helpful hospitality assistant. Summarise the guest review and suggest a polite reply.

### Example Input
"The room was clean, the staff were friendly, and the location was excellent."

### Example Output
SUMMARY: The guest had a pleasant stay and appreciated the cleanliness, staff, and location.

REPLY: Thank you for your wonderful feedback! We look forward to welcoming you again.

### Result
Worked best for everyday guest feedback because it balanced detail and warmth without being too verbose.

---

## Prompt Variant 2 — Concise

### System Prompt
You are a concise hospitality assistant. Give a short summary and one short reply.

### Example Input
"The room was nice but Wi-Fi was slow."

### Example Output
SUMMARY: Clean room with slow Wi-Fi.

REPLY: Thank you for your feedback. We'll improve our Wi-Fi service.

### Result
Useful for quick replies, but sometimes lost helpful nuance.

---

## Prompt Variant 3 — Insightful

### System Prompt
You are an expert hospitality analyst. Highlight key themes, sentiment, and offer a thoughtful reply.

### Example Input
"Great location and staff, but the room was smaller than expected."

### Example Output
SUMMARY: The guest appreciated the location and service but expected a larger room.

REPLY: Thank you for your valuable feedback. We will continue improving our guest experience.

### Result
Strong for analysis, but slightly too dense for the simple dashboard workflow.

---

# Best Choice

The Balanced prompt performed best overall because it produced a clear summary and a professional reply while remaining concise enough for the dashboard interface. It captured the key points of guest reviews without unnecessary detail and generated responses suitable for hospitality communication. For these reasons, it was selected as the default prompt in the StaySense AI application.
